Privacy Policy

Developed by Sheikh Abir Ali

Short version: TimeShift collects absolutely no personal data. All timezone conversions happen entirely on your device. Nothing is ever sent anywhere.

What data we collect

None. TimeShift does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal information, browsing data, or usage data.

How the extension works

TimeShift performs all timezone calculations locally in your browser using JavaScript's built-in Intl API. No external servers, APIs, or network requests are made at any point.

The only data stored is your timezone preferences (your chosen "From" and "To" timezones), which are saved using the browser's built-in storage.sync API. This syncs your preferences across your own devices. Your browser manages this sync — TimeShift never touches or reads this data beyond your own device.

Permissions explained

  • contextMenus — Adds the "Convert Time" option when you right-click selected text.
  • storage — Saves your timezone preferences so you don't have to re-select them each time.
  • activeTab — Allows showing the conversion overlay on the current page after a right-click conversion. We do not read page content.
  • tabs — Required to inject the overlay UI into the page. No page data is read or transmitted.

No analytics, no tracking

TimeShift contains no analytics software, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no third-party code of any kind. The extension is 100% self-contained.

Children's privacy

Since we collect no data, TimeShift is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update it here and bump the extension version. We will never introduce data collection without clearly notifying users first.
